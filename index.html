Our Mission

We are a chess coaching business based in Richmond, Virginia run by a group of high schooler students. We strive to utilize our experience in chess, from playing tournaments to studying positions, to educate upcoming players at all levels. It's our goal to use our passion for chess to teach students while also making an impact on our community as it is a future goal of ours to donate a share of our proceeds to charity!

Our 1-on-1 online lessons use a skill-based pricing system fit for all levels of players! Use our registration page to register your child for our amazing lessons!

Pricing:
$10 for beginner students
$15 for intermediate students
$20 for advanced students

Content

To determine the type of content that we will be teaching your child, the first session will be used to determine the skill level and prior knowledge of your child. Based on what we learn in this session, our team will design an effective learning plan for your child to reach chess success! Parents are free to request any special topics if needed.

Resources

Our classes will be held online through google meet or zoom, depending on your preference. As a result, your child will need to create a "chess.com" or "lichess.org" account to play chess online. While "chess.com" is recommended by our team, lichess.org also works.
